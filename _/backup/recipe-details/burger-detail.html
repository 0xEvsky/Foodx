Classic Beef Burger

Prep Time: 15 mins Cook Time: 10-15 mins Servings: 4 Cuisine: American
Burger Beef Grill Dinner

Overview

The quintessential classic beef burger recipe. Juicy, flavorful patties cooked to perfection on the grill or stovetop, served on a toasted bun with your favorite toppings. Simple, satisfying, and always a hit.

Ingredients

Burger Patties

  • 1.5 lbs Ground beef (80/20 blend recommended)
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • 1/2 tsp Black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp Garlic powder (optional)
  • 1/2 tsp Onion powder (optional)

For Serving

  • 4 Burger buns
  • Cheese slices (cheddar, American, etc.)
  • Lettuce leaves
  • Tomato slices
  • Onion slices (red or white)
  • Pickle slices
  • Ketchup, mustard, mayonnaise

Instructions

1

Prepare Patties

Gently combine ground beef, salt, pepper, and optional garlic/onion powder in a bowl. Do not overmix. Form into 4 equal patties, about 3/4 inch thick. Make a slight indentation in the center of each patty to prevent puffing.

2

Preheat Grill/Pan

Preheat grill to medium-high heat, or heat a cast-iron skillet over medium-high heat.

3

Cook Burgers

Grill or pan-sear patties for 4-5 minutes per side for medium-rare, or longer depending on desired doneness. Add cheese during the last minute of cooking, if desired.

4

Toast Buns

Lightly toast the inside of the burger buns on the grill or in the pan during the last minute of cooking.

5

Assemble and Serve

Place cooked patties on toasted buns. Add desired toppings and condiments. Serve immediately.

Chef's Tips

Handle Gently

Avoid overworking the meat when forming patties for a tender burger.

Don't Press

Resist the urge to press down on patties while cooking; this squeezes out juices.

Check Doneness

Use a meat thermometer for accurate results (160°F/71°C for ground beef is recommended for safety).
