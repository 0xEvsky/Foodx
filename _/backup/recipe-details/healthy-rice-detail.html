Healthy Rice Bowl

Prep Time: 15 mins Cook Time: 20 mins Servings: 2 Cuisine: International
Healthy Bowl Vegetarian Option Lunch

Overview

A customizable and nutritious rice bowl packed with vegetables, protein, and a flavorful dressing. Perfect for a healthy lunch or light dinner, easily adaptable to your favorite ingredients.

Ingredients

Base & Protein

  • 1 cup Cooked brown rice (or quinoa)
  • 1 cup Cooked chickpeas or black beans
  • Optional Grilled chicken, tofu, or salmon

Vegetables

  • 1 cup Spinach or mixed greens
  • 1/2 Avocado, sliced
  • 1/2 cup Cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 Cucumber, diced
  • 1/4 cup Shredded carrots

Dressing/Toppings

  • 2 tbsp Tahini or favorite dressing
  • 1 tbsp Lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp Sesame seeds
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

1

Prepare Base

Divide cooked rice between two bowls.

2

Add Protein & Veggies

Arrange chickpeas/beans, spinach, avocado, tomatoes, cucumber, and carrots over the rice. Add optional cooked protein.

3

Prepare Dressing

If making tahini dressing, whisk tahini, lemon juice, and a little water until smooth. Season with salt and pepper.

4

Dress and Serve

Drizzle bowls with dressing and sprinkle with sesame seeds. Serve immediately.

Chef's Tips

Meal Prep

Cook rice and prepare vegetables ahead of time for quick assembly.

Seasonal Veggies

Use any seasonal vegetables you like - roasted sweet potatoes, broccoli, or bell peppers work well.

Dressing Options

Other great dressings include lemon vinaigrette, peanut sauce, or a simple drizzle of olive oil and balsamic.
