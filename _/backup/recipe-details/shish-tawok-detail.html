Shish Tawook (Chicken Kebab)

Prep Time: 20 mins Marinating Time: 1+ hour Cook Time: 15-20 mins Servings: 4 Cuisine: Middle Eastern
Chicken Grill Kebab Dinner

Overview

Juicy and flavorful Middle Eastern chicken kebabs marinated in yogurt, lemon juice, garlic, and spices. Perfect for grilling or baking, Shish Tawook is a crowd-pleaser.

Ingredients

Chicken & Marinade

  • 1.5 lbs Boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into 1-inch cubes
  • 1/2 cup Plain yogurt
  • 1/4 cup Lemon juice
  • 2 tbsp Olive oil
  • 4 cloves Garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp Tomato paste
  • 1 tsp Paprika
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • 1/2 tsp Black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp Dried oregano

For Skewers (Optional)

  • 1 Bell pepper, cut into chunks
  • 1 Onion, cut into chunks
  • Wooden or metal skewers

Instructions

1

Marinate Chicken

Combine all marinade ingredients in a bowl. Add chicken cubes and toss to coat well. Cover and refrigerate for at least 1 hour, preferably 4 hours or overnight.

2

Assemble Skewers

If using wooden skewers, soak them in water for 30 minutes. Thread marinated chicken onto skewers, alternating with bell pepper and onion chunks if desired.

3

Grill

Preheat grill to medium-high. Grill skewers for 10-15 minutes, turning occasionally, until chicken is cooked through and slightly charred.

4

Rest and Serve

Let chicken rest for 5 minutes before serving. Serve with pita bread, rice, or salad.

Chef's Tips

Marinating Time

Longer marinating allows flavors to penetrate deeper.

Even Cooking

Cut chicken into uniform pieces for even cooking.

Serving Suggestion

Great with garlic sauce (toum).
