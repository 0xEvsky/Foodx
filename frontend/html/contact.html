Frequently Asked Questions

How do I save a recipe to my favorites?

Click the "Add to Favorites" button on any recipe page while logged in to save it to your favorites collection.

Can I submit my own recipes?

Yes! Once logged in, you can submit your recipes through the "Submit Recipe" option in your profile menu.

How do I reset my password?

Click the "Forgot Password" link on the login page and follow the instructions sent to your email.

Are the recipes verified?

All recipes undergo a review process before being published to ensure quality and accuracy.
