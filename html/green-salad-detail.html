Green Salad

Prep Time: 15 mins Cook Time: 0 mins Servings: 4 Cuisine: International
Vegetarian Healthy Side Dish Quick

Overview

A refreshing green salad that combines crisp lettuce, juicy tomatoes, and a variety of fresh vegetables. This versatile dish works as a light lunch on its own or as the perfect side dish to complement any meal. The simple vinaigrette dressing enhances the natural flavors without overwhelming them.

Ingredients

Salad Base

  • 4 cups Mixed greens (lettuce, spinach, arugula)
  • 1 cup Cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 Cucumber, sliced
  • 1 Avocado, diced
  • 1/2 Red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup Feta cheese, crumbled

Dressing

  • 3 tbsp Extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 tbsp Lemon juice
  • 1 tsp Dijon mustard
  • 1/2 tsp Honey
  • To taste Salt and black pepper

Instructions

1

Prepare the Vegetables

Wash all vegetables thoroughly. Tear or chop the lettuce into bite-sized pieces. Slice the cucumber, halve the cherry tomatoes, dice the avocado, and thinly slice the red onion.

2

Make the Dressing

In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil, lemon juice, Dijon mustard, honey, salt, and pepper until well combined and emulsified.

3

Assemble the Salad

In a large bowl, combine the mixed greens, tomatoes, cucumber, avocado, and red onion. Toss gently to mix the ingredients.

4

Finish and Serve

Drizzle the dressing over the salad and toss gently to coat. Sprinkle with crumbled feta cheese. Serve immediately for best freshness and flavor.

Chef's Tips

Freshness

For maximum crispness, chill the salad bowl in the refrigerator before assembling.

Variations

Add grilled chicken, nuts, or dried fruits for extra flavor and protein.

Storage

Store dressing separately if not serving immediately to prevent soggy greens.
