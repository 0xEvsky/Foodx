Refreshing Summer Salad

Prep Time: 20 mins Cook Time: 0 mins Servings: 4-6 Cuisine: International
Salad Healthy Vegetarian Summer

Overview

A vibrant and refreshing summer salad packed with seasonal vegetables, fruits, and a light vinaigrette. Perfect as a side dish for barbecues or a light lunch on a warm day.

Ingredients

Salad Base

  • 5 oz Mixed greens or spinach
  • 1 cup Strawberries, hulled and sliced
  • 1/2 cup Blueberries
  • 1 Avocado, diced
  • 1/2 Cucumber, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup Red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup Crumbled feta or goat cheese
  • 1/4 cup Toasted almonds or pecans

Dressing

  • 3 tbsp Olive oil
  • 2 tbsp Balsamic vinegar
  • 1 tsp Dijon mustard
  • 1 tsp Honey or maple syrup
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

1

Prepare Dressing

In a small bowl or jar, whisk together olive oil, balsamic vinegar, Dijon mustard, honey/maple syrup, salt, and pepper until well combined.

2

Prepare Salad Ingredients

Wash and prepare all fruits and vegetables as described in the ingredients list.

3

Assemble Salad

In a large salad bowl, combine mixed greens, strawberries, blueberries, avocado, cucumber, and red onion.

4

Add Toppings & Dress

Sprinkle feta/goat cheese and toasted nuts over the salad. Drizzle with dressing just before serving and toss gently to combine.

5

Serve

Serve immediately.

Chef's Tips

Add Protein

Grilled chicken or shrimp make a great addition for a heartier meal.

Serve Fresh

Assemble and dress the salad just before serving to prevent wilting.

Nut-Free Option

Use toasted pumpkin seeds or sunflower seeds instead of nuts.
