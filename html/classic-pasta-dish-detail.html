Classic Pasta Dish (e.g., Spaghetti Aglio e Olio)

Prep Time: 5 mins Cook Time: 15 mins Servings: 2-3 Cuisine: Italian
Pasta Italian Quick Vegetarian

Overview

A simple yet incredibly flavorful classic Italian pasta dish, often featuring garlic, olive oil, and red pepper flakes (like Aglio e Olio). Minimal ingredients, maximum taste, ready in under 20 minutes.

Ingredients (Example: Aglio e Olio)

Pasta & Sauce

  • 8 oz Spaghetti or Linguine
  • 1/3 cup Extra virgin olive oil
  • 6-8 cloves Garlic, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 - 1/2 tsp Red pepper flakes (to taste)
  • 1/2 cup Reserved pasta water
  • Salt to taste
  • 1/4 cup Fresh parsley, chopped
  • Optional Grated Parmesan cheese

Instructions (Example: Aglio e Olio)

1

Cook Pasta

Cook pasta in a large pot of generously salted boiling water according to package directions until al dente. Reserve about 1 cup of pasta water before draining.

2

Sauté Garlic

While pasta cooks, heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium-low heat. Add sliced garlic and red pepper flakes. Cook slowly, stirring frequently, for 5-8 minutes until garlic is lightly golden. Do not let it brown or burn.

3

Combine

Add the drained pasta to the skillet with the garlic oil. Add about 1/2 cup of the reserved pasta water. Toss well to coat the pasta, allowing the sauce to emulsify slightly.

4

Finish and Serve

Stir in fresh parsley. Season with salt if needed. Serve immediately, garnished with Parmesan cheese if desired.

Chef's Tips

Watch the Garlic

Cook garlic slowly over low heat to infuse the oil without burning it.

Pasta Water is Key

The starchy pasta water helps create a creamy sauce that clings to the pasta.

Quality Ingredients

Use good quality extra virgin olive oil for the best flavor.
