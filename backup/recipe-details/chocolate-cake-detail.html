Simple Chocolate Cake

Prep Time: 20 mins Cook Time: 35-40 mins Servings: 10 Cuisine: Dessert
Chocolate Cake Dessert Easy Baking

Overview

A straightforward and reliable recipe for a moist, delicious chocolate cake. Perfect for birthdays, potlucks, or just a simple treat. Frost with your favorite chocolate or vanilla buttercream.

Ingredients

Cake

  • 1 1/2 cups All-purpose flour
  • 1 cup Granulated sugar
  • 1/3 cup Unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp Baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp Salt
  • 1 cup Water
  • 1/2 cup Vegetable oil
  • 1 tsp Vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp White vinegar

Optional Frosting

  • 1/2 cup Butter, softened
  • 2 cups Powdered sugar
  • 1/4 cup Cocoa powder
  • 2-3 tbsp Milk
  • 1/2 tsp Vanilla extract

Instructions

1

Preheat & Prepare Pan

Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour an 8x8 inch square baking pan or a 9-inch round cake pan.

2

Mix Dry Ingredients

In a large bowl, whisk together flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking soda, and salt.

3

Mix Wet Ingredients

In a separate bowl, combine water, oil, vanilla extract, and vinegar.

4

Combine

Pour wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and whisk until just combined. Do not overmix.

5

Bake

Pour batter into the prepared pan. Bake for 35-40 minutes, or until a wooden skewer inserted into the center comes out clean.

6

Cool & Frost

Let the cake cool completely in the pan before frosting. To make frosting: beat butter until creamy, gradually add powdered sugar and cocoa, then beat in milk and vanilla until smooth.

Chef's Tips

Vinegar Reaction

The vinegar reacts with baking soda to help the cake rise (no eggs needed in this version).

Mixing

Mix batter only until ingredients are combined; a few lumps are okay.

Variations

Add chocolate chips to the batter or top with sprinkles.
